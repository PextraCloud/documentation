# Audit Trail
To view audit logs:

1. From the left navigation panel, click your **Organization Name**.
2. On the right, click **Audit Log**.
3. The **Audit Log** section displays actions performed by users, providing a full audit trail.

![Audit Trail](./images/audit-logs.png)

>[!NOTE]
>Audit log entries may take up to one minute to appear after an action is performed.
